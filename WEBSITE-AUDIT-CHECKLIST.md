## How to Use This Checklist
1. Open your website in multiple browsers and devices

5. Prioritize fixes based on the priority levels

## NEXT STEPS

### After Completing This Checklist:

1. **Prioritize issues** based on:
   - Impact on conversions
   - Ease of implementation
   - Available resources

2. **Group related fixes** together:
   - All image optimization at once
   - All copy updates together
   - All form improvements together

3. **Create implementation timeline:**
   - Week 1-2: Critical items
   - Week 3-4: High priority items
   - Month 2: Medium priority items

4. **Request specific changes** from development team:
   - Be specific about what needs to change
   - Provide examples where helpful
   - Reference this checklist

5. **Set up tracking** before making changes:
   - Baseline metrics captured?
   - Analytics configured?
   - Goals set up?
